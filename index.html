Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Embracing digital transformation, encouraging tech-based solutions, and creating opportunities for innovation and growth.

Sustainability

Promoting green initiatives, preserving natural resources, and prioritizing sustainable development to combat climate challenges.

Infrastructure

Building efficient transportation, energy, and communication systems to support rapid urbanization and national development.

Empowerment

Enabling individuals, especially women and marginalized groups, with education, resources, and opportunities to contribute to an inclusive and progressive society.
